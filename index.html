Philippines

The Philippines, an archipelago of more than 7,000 islands in Southeast Asia, is renowned for its stunning beaches, rich biodiversity, and warm hospitality. From the famous Banaue Rice Terraces to the crystal-clear waters of Boracay and Palawan, its sceneries are diverse. The Philippines offers a vibrant mix of natural beauty, history, and cultural richness that captivates visitors and locals alike.
