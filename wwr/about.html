Dry Oar Rafting Co.

Our mission is to navigate the thrilling waves of adventure with safety and enthusiasm. We are committed to creating unforgettable experiences for our clients through the power of teamwork and a love for the outdoors.

History

Founded in 2001 by John Smith, Dry Oar Rafting Co. quickly became known for its exhilarating river adventures in Colorado, USA. John's expertise as a river guide and his commitment to safety and customer satisfaction helped the company grow rapidly.

By 2010, a talented team, including Aisha Khan in Customer Service, Michael Brown in Operations, and Emily Davis as Safety Officer, joined the mission to provide unforgettable rafting experiences.

Today, Dry Oar Rafting Co. is a leader in adventure tourism, offering a variety of rafting trips for all skill levels. Embracing innovation and technology, the company continues to inspire adventurers worldwide, ensuring every trip is both exciting and safe.

From serene float trips to adrenaline-pumping whitewater adventures, Dry Oar Rafting Co. remains dedicated to sharing the thrill of the river with enthusiasts everywhere.
